Disk Scheduling

Disk scheduling is done by operating systems to schedule I/O requests arriving for the disk. Disk scheduling is also known as I/O scheduling. To compare the performance (on the basis of total seek time) of the different disk scheduling algorithms, select Compare in the Algorithm drop-down list.
